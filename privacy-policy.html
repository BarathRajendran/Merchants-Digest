Last updated: January 1, 2026

Merchants Digest ("we", "our", "us") operates a newsletter focused on insights, analysis, and educational content for Shopify merchants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you subscribe to or interact with Merchants Digest.

By subscribing to Merchants Digest, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information necessary to operate and improve the newsletter.

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you subscribe to Merchants Digest, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Company or store name (optional)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with our website or emails, we may collect:

  • IP address

  • Email open and click activity

This data is collected through standard analytics and email service tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Deliver newsletter emails and updates
  • Improve newsletter content and relevance
  • Monitor engagement (opens, clicks)
  • Communicate important updates related to Merchants Digest
  • Prevent abuse, spam, or unauthorized use

We do not sell or rent subscriber data to third parties.

3. Email Communications

By subscribing, you consent to receive emails from Merchants Digest. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in every email.

Transactional or operational emails (such as confirmation emails) may still be sent even if you opt out of marketing emails.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure storage via reputable email and analytics providers

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate Merchants Digest, such as:

  • Email delivery platforms

These services may process limited personal data on our behalf and are required to comply with applicable data protection standards.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain subscriber information only for as long as necessary to operate the newsletter or comply with legal obligations. If you unsubscribe, your data may be removed or anonymized within a reasonable period.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications

You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the details below.

Merchants Digest is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
